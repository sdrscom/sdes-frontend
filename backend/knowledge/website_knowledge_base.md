## FAQ Answers
* **Establishing a branch:** You do not have to establish a branch inside the zone to practice business[cite: 2].
* **Customs Duty on Re-exports:** Custom duty is not paid for cargo kept in the zone for re-export; it is only applicable for cargo released to the local Saudi market[cite: 2].
* **Storage Time limit:** The allowable storage time period is three years[cite: 2].
* **Port Charges:** Port charges are not applicable upon goods destined to the zone[cite: 2].
* **Manufacturing Duty:** Custom duty is not applicable upon local or imported materials for manufacturing inside the zone[cite: 2].
